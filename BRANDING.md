# Eqavo Branding

`Eqavo` is the working product name for this unofficial macOS-first Chinese localization project based on Zed source code.

## Positioning

- Unofficial
- macOS-first
- local-first
- Chinese-localized
- not affiliated with Zed Industries

## Naming guidance

Use:

- `Eqavo`
- `Eqavo for macOS`
- `Eqavo Editor`

Do not use:

- `Official Zed Chinese`
- `Zed CN Official`
- Zed's official logo or app icon

## Tone

- clean
- calm
- technical
- native on macOS

## Visual direction

- Rounded square app icon
- Deep teal to near-black gradient
- Bright cyan accent
- Geometric `E` form built from editor-frame lines
- Minimal, sharp, readable at small sizes
